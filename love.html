Using the Scanner

The scanner only works for barcodes that are black with a white background. The scanner only reads UPC codes. Try scanning the barcode again if the barcode does not match with result given.

Scanner Result

The result of the scanner is based on the Dirty Dozen additives. We will alert you if it contains any of the Dirty Dozen additives. Look at the significance of the different feebacks below. Click here to Learn more.

Problems

If there is a problem with the scanner please try scanning it again or contact us.

Products

Our database of product is limted so if is not in our database then a feeback cannot be given. We are currently growing our database.

Significance of HARMFUL ADDITIVE(S) DETECTED

We recommend you reconsider consuming this product or only consume this product in moderation.

Significance of NO ADDITIVES DETECTED

There are no food additives from the Dirty Dozen list in this product. Depending on your nutritional needs and dietary restrictions, there may be other ingredients in this product that could impact your health.
